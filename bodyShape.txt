Rectangle Body Shape: Shoulder and hip widths are approximately equal, and the waist is not significantly narrower. The body appears straight with minimal curves.
Pear Body Shape: Hips are wider than shoulders, and the waist is typically narrow. Most weight is concentrated in the lower body (hips and thighs).
Apple Body Shape: Shoulders are broader than hips, and most weight is carried in the upper body (stomach and bust).The waist may be narrower than the upper body but is not distinctly defined.
Hourglass Body Shape: Shoulders and hips are approximately equal, but the waist is narrow and well-defined. The body has noticeable curves at the waist, giving it a balanced appearance.
